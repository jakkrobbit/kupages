KU Bookstore Pages

As a web coordinator for the University of Kansas Bookstore, one of my responsibilities included creating additional pages and sections to their official site. To do this, I utilized HTML, CSS, Javascript, and, in a few cases, SQL.

This site was created so the various elements I designed could be showcase as live, working pages, in case they are altered or removed from the official site. So the designs can be showcased in the same way they appeared at the time of development, I've used the same layout as the original site.

Click any of the options below to see pages I've made. Here's the official KU Bookstore site for comparison.
